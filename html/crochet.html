CROCHET PROJECTS

Grey Mittens

Crafted from grey acrylic yarn, these mittens have a beautiful textured design while also being warm and comfortable. The mittens are also lined inside with fleece for extra warmth and finished with ribbed cuff detailings.

  • Completed on November 13, 2022
Progress
  • Working on the finishing second mitten after completing the first, while the first mitten is only missing the thumb.
  • For both mittens I have completed the finger tips all the way to the cuffs, the next step is to add the thumbs.
  • Here I completed the thumb and attatched it to the mitten by sewing the crocheted thumb to the hole in the mitten.
Resources
  • Youtube Tutorial by The Turtle Trunk on How to Crochet Country Mittens
  • Grey Acrylic Yarn
White Lap Blanket

Crafted in an off-white hue, this lap blanket is crocheted using the diagonal basket weave stitch. Fashioned from cozy acrylic yarn in the Natural shade, it offers both warmth and a luxuriously comfortable feel.

  • Completed on December 1, 2023
Progress
  • Blanket was one fifth of the way done. Used one entire ball of yarn to get to this point.
  • In this image I am in the process of crocheting my blanket while lying in bed. The blanket was about three fifths of the way complete.
  • On to the final and fifth ball of yarn for the blanket. In total it took five balls of yarn to complete the lap blanket. The blanket fits comfortably on my lap while I work at my desk.
Resources
  • Youtube Tutorial by Mama In The Stitch on How To Crochet Diagonal Basket Weave Blanket
  • Natural Colour Acrylic Yarn
